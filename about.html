Corporate Profile

In 2007 we have forayed into the cartridge refilling and printing solutions to major corporate houses and individuals and 2008 we have made our new brand name PRINT STUDIO (INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED (PRINT STUDIO) providing complete cost effective solutions in cartridge refilling and printing solutions industry. With the backing of a knowledge bank of providing varied expertise in the service sector for a range of industries & institutions, PRINT STUDIO is able to offer you a range of services to help you to earn a good business prospects by asking others reduce their daily printing expenditure.
